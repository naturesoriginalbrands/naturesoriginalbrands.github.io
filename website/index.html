About the Calculator

Lorem ipsum. Informational paragraph about the dog food calculator tool. In this paragraph we discuss the Resting Energy Requirements of dogs, and why someone should use our tool.
Note: we are not veterinarians, and the information on this page does not represent the opinion of an expert. Please consult a dietician before making decisions about your pet's daily food intake.

Some related facts:

Pets' RER (Resting Energy Requirements) depend on weight, neutered/intact status, breed, and age.

These calculations can only give a "ballpark" estimate: adjusting in the long term the amount of food you feed your pet will necessarily depend on personalized attention to a pet's needs.

Muscle condition, as well as coat and skin quality, are affected by the amount of protein that a pet is getting. If a pets has a healthy muscle condition score, and good skin and coat quality, that is an indication that they are getting enough protein.
